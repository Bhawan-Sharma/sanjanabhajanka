Sanjana Bhajanka

Introducing Sanjana Bhajanka, our super multitasking wonder! Not your average superhero, she's India's first certified baby planner, proudly earning her cape (or should we say certification) from the International Parenting and Health Institute in California, alongside the IABPP team in June 2020.
But wait, there's more! Before conquering the world of parenting, she aced the world of law at the University of Warwick, England, in 2010. By 2011, she was a barrister, a proud member of the prestigious Lincoln's Inn, London.
Now, her primary mission is guiding soon-to-be parents and newbies on their thrilling journey into parenthood. She's all about empowerment through education, offering valuable resources and being the wind beneath those parenting wings.
But that's not all, folks!
Did we mention she's a mother of two? Yes, she juggles life, parenthood, marketing, and her latest endeavor, changing the world, one superheroic feat at a time, with her own NGO in Calcutta, the Live Strong Foundation. It's a beacon of hope for underprivileged cancer kids, and she's leading the charge.
Sanjana Bhajanka—our real-life superhero, making the world a better place while being an incredible mother of two!
